0–6 Years: Early Childhood

This is a crucial time for your child's growth and development.

Early Childhood (0-6 years)

Physical Development

  • Help your child develop gross motor skills (crawling, walking).
  • Create a safe space for active exploration.
  • Ensure a regular sleep schedule and a balanced diet.

Cognitive Development

  • Talk to your child, read books, sing songs.
  • Stimulate sensory perception (play with textures, sounds, colors).
  • Develop cause-and-effect thinking through simple games: hide and seek.

Emotional development

  • Be sensitive to the child's needs: comfort, hug, smile.
  • Teach to distinguish basic emotions: joy, sadness, fear.
  • Create a stable and predictable environment.
